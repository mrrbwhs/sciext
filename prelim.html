Preliminary Course – Investigating Science

Module 1: Cause and Effect - Observing and Experimenting

Explore how observations form the basis of scientific inquiry and how cause-and-effect relationships are investigated through controlled experimentation.

Module 2: Cause and Effect - Inferences and Generalisations

Investigate how inferences and generalisations are drawn from experimental results and how they can lead to scientific laws, models and theories.

Module 3: Scientific Models

Examine the role of models in science, their limitations, and how they are used to represent and test scientific ideas.

Module 4: Theories and Laws

Differentiate between scientific theories and laws, and consider how they are supported or challenged by evidence over time.

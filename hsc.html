Science Extension Syllabus Modules

Module 1: The Foundations of Scientific Thinking

Explore historic and cultural observations, the way scientific research has changed over time, and how ethical frameworks have influenced this process. Consider philosophical arguments in relation to the nature of science to deepen your understanding and refine your own views of and about science.

Module 2: The Scientific Research Proposal

Learn to develop a question that enables scientific research by exploring peer-reviewed published literature for guidance. Undertake relevant scientific research and develop a detailed plan that lays the foundations for the subsequent processes to be employed to address a developed hypothesis.

Module 3: The Data, Evidence and Decisions

Explore the difference between the concepts of proving that something is true and measuring the probability of a certain result by understanding falsification and statistical levels of confidence.

Module 4: The Research Report

Assists in making final additions to the related Scientific Research Portfolio and to complete the related Scientific Research Report.
